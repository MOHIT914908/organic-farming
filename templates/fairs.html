Participation in Fairs & Exhibition.

UOCB participated in the different exhibitions and fairs at state/ national level.
In these exhibitions and fairs facilitate the sale of the organic commodity directly by the farmers group.
Also promotion and awareness of organic farming and product are done through it.
In year 2019 UOCB has been participated in 35 Fair and Exhibitions from where participated farmers were sold their produce of value
Rs. 28 Lakh and also collected demand of different commodities of Value Rs. 210 Lakh.

In year 2020-21 UOCB participated in 11 Fair and Exhibitions, where farmers sold their produce of value Rs. 08 Lakh and collected demand of Rs. 85 Lakh of spices And Culinary Herbs
